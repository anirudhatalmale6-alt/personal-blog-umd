About Duluth

Duluth, Minnesota is a vibrant city located at the western tip of Lake Superior. Known for its scenic beauty, outdoor adventures, and thriving arts scene, Duluth is the perfect college town. From hiking the Superior Hiking Trail to exploring Canal Park, there's always something to discover.

The city offers a unique blend of natural beauty and urban amenities, making it an ideal place for students who want the best of both worlds.
